Options for statistical cleaning of measurements, to discard extreme values.

The Keep Factor parameter defines the interval for valid measurements, based on the following principle:

  • let m be the measurements mean
  • let e be the standard deviation
  • let f be the Keep Factor
Retained values belong to interval ]m - f*e, m + f*e[.

The Keep Percentage parameter allows for limiting the clean-up operation, to keep a sufficient amount of measurements.
